Green Horizons Youth Network

Building Climate Resilience Through Youth Action

About Us

Green Horizons Youth Network is a community-focused non-profit organisation dedicated to advancing climate resilience through education and practical action. We empower youth and communities with the knowledge and tools to adapt to climate change, protect natural resources, and foster a sustainable future.

What We Do

  • Climate education and school outreach programs
  • Community engagement and awareness workshops
  • Youth-led climate action initiatives
  • Establishment of climate school clubs

Our Impact

We have engaged over 500 youth and community members through climate education programs and conducted 12 community workshops. Five climate school clubs have been established, reaching approximately 100 learners.
